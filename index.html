Augustinas Jacovskis

I'm a first year mathematics PhD student at the University of Edinburgh. My supervisor is Prof. Arend Bayer. I am a member of the School of Mathematics, as well as the Hodge Institute.

Research interests: Algebraic geometry, more specifically derived categories of coherent sheaves, Bridgeland stability conditions, wall-crossing, moduli spaces.

At the moment, I'm thinking about stability conditions on Kuznetsov components, and applications to categorical Torelli theorems involving the Kuznetsov component. I'm also working on glued stability conditions on del Pezzo surfaces via semiorthogonal decompositions of their derived categories.

Background: Before coming to Edinburgh, I did a master's degree at Oxford, and a bachelor's degree at Durham.
